## <span class="lang-en">Overview</span><span class="lang-zh">概述</span>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
Legged robots operating in disaster-rescue environments must traverse terrains with conflicting geometric demands: narrow low-clearance passages favor compact limbs, while stairs and slopes benefit from longer reach. Since no fixed morphology performs well across all tasks, we propose **CALA** — a hierarchical bi-level co-design framework for terrain-specific adaptive locomotion.
</div>

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
在灾难救援场景中作业的腿足机器人需要穿越具有相互冲突几何需求的地形：狭窄低净空通道需要紧凑肢体，而台阶与坡面则需要更长的臂展。由于没有任何固定形态能在所有任务中表现最佳，我们提出 **CALA**——一种面向地形自适应运动的分层双层协同设计框架。
</div>

## <span class="lang-en">Key Ideas</span><span class="lang-zh">核心方法</span>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">

**Unified Parametric Morphology Model**  
Telescopic prismatic joints in a single physics asset enable continuous limb-length variation, eliminating per-candidate model regeneration during search.

**Bi-Level Co-Optimization**  
- **Outer loop (CMA-ES):** Searches over continuous morphology space M.
- **Inner loop (PPO fine-tuning):** Rapidly adapts morphology-agnostic prior π₀ with only K ≪ K₀ iterations.

**Morphology-Randomized Pretraining**  
Pretraining π₀ under randomized morphology m ~ p(m) yields a transferable prior that substantially reduces inner-loop budget, validated across two robot platforms.

</div>

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">

**统一参数化形态模型**  
单一物理资产中内置伸缩式棱柱关节，实现连续肢体长度变化，消除形态搜索过程中逐候选模型重建的开销。

**双层协同优化**  
- **外层（CMA-ES）：** 在连续形态空间 M 上搜索最优形态。
- **内层（PPO 微调）：** 以仅需 K ≪ K₀ 次迭代的低代价，快速将形态无关先验策略 π₀ 适配到当前候选形态。

**形态随机化预训练**  
在随机形态 m ~ p(m) 下预训练 π₀，获得跨形态可迁移先验，显著降低内层微调预算，在两个机器人平台上均获验证。

</div>

## <span class="lang-en">Experiments</span><span class="lang-zh">实验</span>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">

**Robot platforms:**
- Unitree Go2 — telescopic thigh & calf extensions
- Ant — telescopic leg & foot extensions

**Terrain types:** Go2: Ring Crawl · Rough Climb · Random Boxes ｜ Ant: Corridor · Slope · Rough

**Results:**
- Morphology-randomized pretraining (Group A) outperforms fixed-morphology pretraining and scratch training across all six terrain–platform combinations
- CMA-ES discovers geometrically interpretable terrain-specific morphologies (compact for Ring Crawl; extended for Rough Climb)
- **Adaptive-Switch** achieves the highest traversal distance (35.2 ± 1.6 m) and fitness (0.52–0.56) on composite sequential terrains

</div>

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">

**机器人平台：**
- Unitree Go2 — 大腿与小腿伸缩关节
- Ant — 腿部与足部伸缩关节

**地形类型：** Go2：环形爬行 · 粗糙攀爬 · 随机箱体 ｜ Ant：走廊 · 坡面 · 粗糙地面

**主要结果：**
- 形态随机化预训练（A组）在全部六种地形–平台组合上持续优于固定形态预训练和从零训练基线
- CMA-ES 发现具有几何可解释性的地形特定形态（环形通道→紧凑肢体；粗糙攀爬→伸长肢体）
- **自适应切换**策略在复合序列地形上实现最优穿越距离（35.2 ± 1.6 m）与适应性得分（0.52–0.56）

</div>
